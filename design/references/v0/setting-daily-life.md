**The Evening Return**

As light fades, the settlement contracts. Building-symbionts begin closing their outer
passages, drawing residents toward protected cores. The evening meal happens in stages:

1. Torrnek prepare the physical components - crushing, mixing, arranging
2. Kemerin add the enzymatic preparations that break down proteins
3. Sethrem manage the precise timing of consumption before compounds degrade

Night brings different hazards - some predator specimens become active, syllabic
emanations grow unstable, and the building-symbionts' immune responses sometimes
mistake residents for threats if they move wrong.

### What Makes This Playable

Players can think "I need to buy supplies" and know:

```
● Go to the Market Pools
● Check the Fresh Rings first for today's specimens
● Bring something to trade (currency is literally living barter)
● Respect the pool protocols (keep specimens wet)
```
They can think "I need information" and know:

```
● The Heights for anything scholarly
● Workways for practical craft knowledge
● Damp Quarter for social gossip (bathhouses are universal)
```
